About Us

Partnering with Omaha Parks and Recreation, Turner Dance offers professional certified dance instruction at an affordable price to children ages 3-12 in the areas of ballet, tap, jazz, hip hop, step team and tumbling. We also offer the always popular Summer Dance Camp for children ages 4-12 each July. Children as young as 2 1/2 may be enrolled with teacher's permission.

Turner Dance has two locations, so every child has lessons within reach: The Saddlebrook Community Center, at 144th and Fort; and the Florence Community Center, at 30th and Florence.
