About Us

Partnering with Omaha Parks and Recreation, Turner Dance offers professional certified dance instruction at an affordable price to children ages 3-12 in the areas of ballet, tap, jazz, hip hop, step team and tumbling. We also offer the always popular Summer Dance Camp for children ages 4-12 each July. Children as young as 2 1/2 may be enrolled with teacher's permission.

Turner Dance has two locations, so every child has lessons within reach: The Saddlebrook Community Center, at 144th and Fort; and the Florence Community Center, at 30th and Florence.

Diane Turner, Director

Diane Turner, a veteran dance instructor and performer, began her professional training at the Omaha Academy of Ballet at age 8. She apprenticed and danced with the Omaha Ballet Company by age 14 and majored in Dance at Creighton Univerity. She studied and trained at New York's premiere dance studio, Steps On Broadway, and graduated from the Royal Academy of Dance. Ms. Turner holds a teacher's certification with the Royal Academy of Dance as well as Dance Masters of America. She also holds her Nebraska teaching license and, for the past 20 years, has substituted with Bellevue Public and Omaha Catholic Schools. Professional performances include shows at the "Upstairs Dinner Theatre", TV commercials, "The Nutcracker" and most recently as an extra in the movie "Up in the Air."
